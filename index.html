Why Do we Do What we Do?


We believe that a child should have every right to what they want in their childhood and our toys can give them a good amount of enjoyment and we love pleasing others. To see others be happy because of us is a huge achievement and is the key to our success!

What is "TOYOS"?

We are a company based in Hong Kong, China and we aim to sell toys which we believe are the fundamental things in the beginning of a toddler/child's boyhood. We were founded in January 2019 by a man called Atindrya Ghosh. Through our toys we inspire creativity and spark curiosity in children. Our toys range from many different sizes and more products will be coming soon as the season changes throughout the year. Keep a lookout for when we have new products! Happy searching!
